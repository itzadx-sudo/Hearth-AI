What Hearth AI does

A complete picture of every patient, continuously

Every feature is built around a single goal: give care teams the right information at the right time, with the confidence to act on it.

1

7-Day Risk PredictionTemporal trajectory forecasting

Hearth AI doesn't just flag today's vitals. It predicts each patient's health trajectory seven days out, using historical patterns to identify who is trending toward decline before it's too late.

2

Multivariate Vital AnalysisSix simultaneous signals

Heart rate, SpO₂, systolic and diastolic BP, body temperature, and activity level are analysed together, not in isolation. The model learns the relationships between them, catching patterns that single-metric thresholds miss.

3

False Alarm ReductionConfidence-gated alerts

Critical predictions are verified against model confidence before firing. Low-confidence Critical alerts are automatically downgraded to Unhealthy, dramatically reducing alarm fatigue without silencing genuinely urgent events.

4

Live Fleet OverviewAll patients. One screen.

The overview dashboard shows every patient's current status, risk percentile, and recent alert in a single leaderboard view, automatically kept current. Filter by status to zero in on critical cases.

IoT-Native, Sensor-Resilient

Hearth AI ingests real-time data from bedside sensors over a persistent socket connection. Missing or failed sensors are handled gracefully. Readings with incomplete vitals are flagged, not silently dropped, and the model adjusts predictions accordingly.

How Hearth AI works

Three stages, running continuously, from raw sensor data to actionable risk assessment.

1

Ingest & Log

IoT sensors stream vitals over a persistent TCP socket. Each reading is timestamped and logged per patient into a local database, including SpO₂, heart rate, blood pressure, temperature, and activity.

2

Infer & Predict

A trained neural network runs inference on each patient's latest readings and 7-day history, producing a risk score and confidence value. Low-confidence critical results are downgraded automatically.

3

Surface & Alert

The dashboard updates continuously with the latest risk scores, status labels, and feature-importance breakdowns. Alerts are generated for status changes and streamed to the alert feed in real time.
